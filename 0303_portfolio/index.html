About Me

The person
behind the
work.

I'm a UX/UI Designer with a strong background in Media Informatics, combining thoughtful design with a solid understanding of technology. My work focuses on creating usable, engaging and inclusive interfaces that connect people across contexts, cultures, and abilities.

Whether collaborating in interdisciplinary teams or working independently, I value structured thinking, open communication, and design decisions grounded in user needs. I enjoy turning complex ideas into intuitive experiences. I'm always curious to explore new tools, methods, and continue learning.

FAQ

Questions I
get asked
often.

What is my design process?
My process starts with understanding the problem, the users, and the context. I move from research to ideation, wireframing, and prototyping — continuously iterating based on feedback.
What does M.Sc. Media Informatics include?
My masters combine Computer Science with Human-Centered Design, covering HCI, UX/UI Design, Software Development, IT Security, AI and IT Ethics — bridging the gap between design and development.
What kind of role am I looking for?
I'm currently exploring UX/UI Designer opportunities with organizations that value global collaboration and take a thoughtful, user-centered approach to designing impactful experiences.
What makes me stand out?
I am more than a Designer. I combine UX thinking, visual design, and a solid understanding of development — creating solutions that are user-centered, visually engaging, and technically feasible.
