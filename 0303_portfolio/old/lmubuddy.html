LMU Buddy App.

A mobile application that improves the LMU Buddy Program by using AI-driven matching and offers tools for planning, communication, and cultural exchange between local and international students.

July - October 2025 Privat Project
Existing Project

What is the LMU Buddy Program.

The LMU Buddy Program pairs international exchange students with local LMU students, helping newcomers settle into life in Munich while fostering cultural exchange, social support, and long-lasting friendships. All students fill out an online form with basic details. Pair matching and notifications are made manually by the LMU team. From there, most communication moves to private messaging platforms like WhatsApp.

Project Goal

The goal of creating the LMU Buddy App.

Design a secure and user-friendly app that:

  • Provides exchange and local students with tools to communicate and explore together
  • Improves buddy matching using AI
  • Strengthens the sense of community and cultural exchange at LMU
  • Ensures data privacy and safety through campus verification and minimal data sharing
My own experience

The problems I found within the existing program.

I participated in the LMU Buddy Program three times, supporting a total of five exchange students from different countries over different semesters. While I genuinely found the program rewarding and valuable, especially for cultural exchange, I noticed several consistent pain points that affected both me and the buddies I was matched with.

Positive Effects
  • share cultural experiences - e.g. introducing my buddies to German food, customs, and every day life in Munich
  • chance to offer insider tips: European travel advice, local hangouts, local shops etc.
  • meaningful way to meet new people from very different backgrounds
Negative Effects

  • Unclear Matching process: currently often different interests, study fields and personalities => harder to help / build a strong connection

    • Time & Scheduling Conflicts

  • hard to meet regularly because of different schedules
  • struggle to offer alternatives or introduce them to other students - no network connection

    • Support Expectations
  • no chance for support before arrival - late contact connection
  • hard to help with administrative processes, or class-related questions — different faculty or just for exchange students
    • Lack of Community Features
  • no central group chats existed - limited group engagement
  • missing group excursions or events - no way to meet other buddies or form group connections / community sense
  • own friend groups - interest loss after initial few meetings
Personal Takeaways
    I wished I had tools to:
  • get in contact early on before the semester starts
  • matched by interest and course as well
  • with other buddy pairs or exchange students too - more free organized activity options
  • offer support without being the only point of contact
Current Website

What the application website currently looks like.

Only for locals (exchange students in application already): Term, Full Name, E-Mail, Gender, Age, Course Information, Languages, Number of Buddy's, Countries of Origin Preferences, First Time as Buddy?
Target Group

Who this platform serves.

  • Primary Users: Exchange students at LMU Munich looking for support, guidance, and local connections
  • Secondary Users: Local LMU students volunteering as buddies to support international peers
  • shared goals of making new friends, exploring Munich and Bavaria, and building a diverse, welcoming community
